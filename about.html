About

WattTheVolt is a blog about starting from scratch - and wiring up a new future.


I'm 33, switching careers, and learning to become an electrician in the UK through hands-on training and hard-earned lessons. This space documents my real, unfiltered journey - from study notes and tool reviews to failed attempts and first wins.


Whether you're a fellow trainee, a career changer, or a curious DIYer, you're welcome here. I'm no expert - just someone figuring it out one circuit at a time, and sharing everything I learn to help others do the same.


If you're starting late, learning fast, or just wondering if you can pull it off, this blog is proof that you can.
